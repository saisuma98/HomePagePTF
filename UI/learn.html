OUR OUTLOOK

DEFORESTATION

We traced that the need for construction is one of the main reasons for cutting down the trees.
Construction of flyovers harms a huge tree population.
This prompts us with the root cause - TRAFFIC.

TRAFFIC PREDICTION

A dataset of 20 years of traffic density in every area has been taken.
Based on the pattern of growth, we predict the traffic density in any year in the near future.
It helps you analyse the need for construction and thus level of Deforestation

TREES & ARABLE LANDS

Once we know the area of attack, we will spot the trees in danger using Object Detection techniques on Satellite Images.
No.of trees to be cut will be estimated.
We will also identify arable lands through the YOLO algorithm.
The same or more no.of saplings will be planted in the arable land in that same area.
Thus,by the time the trees get cut down,the saplings will grow.Thereby striking an Oxygen balance.
